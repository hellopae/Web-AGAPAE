Guān Yī : 观音

The story goes that. The Guan Yin Bodhisattva was incarnated into the human world some ten thousand years ago. She is the daughter of King Miao Zhuang of the Qingling Empire Even though the father treats his daughter badly but with gratitude in remembrance of her parents she knew that his father was sick. Guan Yin Bodhisattva returned to give up her arms and eyes to heal her father from illness. she set up a prayer for her arms and eyes restored to the original, causing the parents, as well as the courtiers who followed her very much to rejoice. and asked to study the teachings of the Buddha.
